# Brand DNA

## One Sentence

Bright ocean air, friendly momentum, blue-haired surfer girl IP, clean white space, blue/pink/orange/white brand color, optimistic but not childish, explanatory but not PPT-like.

## Visual Position

This brand system is a warm personal creator/educator asset. It should feel like a capable surfer-guide helping people cross waves of information, not like a stock beach travel poster.

Preserve Ian Xiaohei's useful logic:

- one image explains one cognitive action
- the character participates in the core mechanism
- short handwritten Chinese annotations help the image read
- the composition has enough silence and whitespace

Replace Ian Xiaohei's visual surface:

- no black blob creature
- no mostly black-and-white sketch look as the final identity
- no weird darkness as the main mood
- no exact reuse of Ian examples

## Theme Colors

Use these as brand anchors. Do not use every color at equal intensity.

- Sky blue: `#48B0E0` / `#60B8E8` for open sky, water, air, light paths.
- Deep ocean blue: `#003080` / `#0B2F7A` for wetsuit, structure lines, key text, stable base.
- Surf orange: `#E08850` / `#F07A2A` for surfboard, action arrows, emphasis, momentum.
- Soft pink: `#F4A7B8` / `#F8C7D2` for cheeks, warmth, small emotional marks, soft highlights.
- Cloud white: `#FFFFFF` / `#F8F8F0` for background, clouds, spacious areas, negative space.

Optional neutrals:

- gentle skin peach: `#F0DCC8`
- pale sand: `#F5E2B8`
- soft cloud gray-blue: `#C8D8E0`

## Color Roles

- Deep ocean blue: main outlines, labels, framework edges, wetsuit, contrast.
- Sky blue: ambient field, water, cloud shadows, secondary systems, calm status.
- Surf orange: main direction, movement, key object, "from here to there" energy.
- Soft pink: emotional cue, encouragement, tiny emphasis, warm human tone.
- White: background and breathing room.

Keep the palette fresh and bright. Use orange as the energetic accent, not as a full-page wash. Use pink sparingly so the design stays clear rather than sweet.

## Required Style Traits

- 16:9 horizontal article illustration unless the user requests otherwise.
- Clean white or airy white-sky background.
- Main subject occupies about 40%-60% of the frame.
- 25%-40% visible whitespace.
- Soft 3D clay/avatar feel is allowed, but simplify the scene into an explanatory illustration.
- Short Chinese annotations, preferably 2-8 characters each.
- Visual metaphor must be understandable without a long caption.

## Avoid

- generic beach poster
- influencer portrait with no conceptual structure
- dense PPT infographic
- corporate process diagram
- childish sticker pack
- overly glossy fashion doll
- dark cyber/tech palette
- beige-only or orange-only palette
- complex UI screenshot
- long Chinese paragraphs inside the image
