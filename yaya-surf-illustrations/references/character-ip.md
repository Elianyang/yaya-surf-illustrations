# Character IP

## Role Definition

Yaya's surfer girl is the fixed visual IP for this skill. She is not a decorative avatar. She should be the person doing the key work: guiding, carrying, sorting, balancing, connecting, rescuing, mapping, catching a wave, or turning a difficult idea into a visible path.

## Portrait Anchors

Use `assets/character-reference.png` for visual calibration.

Core features:

- young woman, friendly and energetic
- vivid blue hair
- one ponytail/braid only, with orange braided accents
- the hairstyle should read as a single gathered ponytail/braid, not twin ponytails, double braids, or two side tails
- expressive large eyes, gentle smile, warm cheeks
- navy/deep-blue wetsuit with white panels
- orange surfboard as a signature object
- blue/pink/orange/white palette
- bright seaside, sky, cloud, water, or clean white-space environment
- a small bottom-corner handwritten credit: `Co-Created with 丫丫老师AI研习社`

The exact lettering on the wetsuit is not important and should not be copied. Avoid generating unreadable brand text on clothing unless the user provides a real logo.

## Personality

- optimistic, capable, curious
- like a guide who knows how to read waves
- warm but not sugary
- brave and practical
- active, in motion, helping ideas move forward
- more "coach and navigator" than "idol portrait"

## Useful Actions

Let the character carry the core action:

- ride a wave that represents a process
- hold a surfboard as bridge, shield, sign, lever, or map
- sort floating cards, shells, buoys, bottles, or notes
- pull a rope between scattered ideas
- rescue a sinking concept
- mark a route across waves
- balance on a board between two choices
- push a tide gate open
- collect evidence pearls or shells
- turn a rough wave into a smooth path
- stand at a lighthouse-style checkpoint
- dive under noisy surface information to retrieve the real insight

## Signature Objects

Use only 1-3 per image:

- orange surfboard
- wave ribbon
- cloud shapes
- buoys
- rope
- shells or evidence pearls
- tide chart
- lighthouse
- small dock
- beach sign
- waterproof notebook
- floating sticky notes

## Avoid

- standing in the corner and waving while the diagram does the work
- exact pose copying from the reference image every time
- turning the image into a pure selfie or portrait
- oversexualized wetsuit styling
- complex costumes or unrelated accessories
- giving the character random logos or malformed English text
- losing the blue hair and orange surfboard identity
- drawing two ponytails, twin braids, or two symmetric side tails
- omitting the bottom-corner credit when generating final artwork

## Character Test

If removing Yaya from the image leaves the idea fully intact, rewrite the prompt. The character should be necessary to the visual metaphor.
